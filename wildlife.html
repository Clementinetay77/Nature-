Animals in Nature

Wildlife refers to animals living in their natural habitats. These animals play important roles in maintaining ecological balance and healthy ecosystems.

Different species depend on one another for survival. Predators control populations, pollinators help plants reproduce, and decomposers recycle nutrients.

Many wildlife species face threats from habitat destruction, pollution, and climate change. Protecting wildlife ensures biodiversity for future generations.
