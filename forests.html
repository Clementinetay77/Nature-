The Importance of Forests

Forests cover large areas of land and are home to countless species of plants and animals. They produce oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, and help regulate Earth's climate.

Forests prevent soil erosion and help maintain the water cycle. Many communities depend on forests for food, medicine, fuel, and building materials.

Tropical rainforests are among the most diverse ecosystems on Earth. Protecting forests is essential for maintaining biodiversity and combating climate change.
