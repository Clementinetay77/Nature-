Welcome to Nature

Nature is one of the most valuable gifts on Earth. It includes forests, mountains, rivers, oceans, plants, animals, and all living organisms. Nature provides beauty, resources, and conditions necessary for life. Every breath we take depends on the natural environment around us.

The natural world inspires people through its landscapes and biodiversity. From towering mountains to colorful flowers, nature offers endless wonders. It also supports agriculture, supplies fresh water, and helps regulate climate.

Protecting nature is important because human survival depends on healthy ecosystems. Understanding nature helps us appreciate its value and encourages responsible environmental practices.
